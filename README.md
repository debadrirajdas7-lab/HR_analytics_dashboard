📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee data to understand workforce trends and identify key factors contributing to employee attrition. An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard was built using Microsoft Power BI to convert raw HR data into meaningful insights.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of employee statistics, attrition patterns, departmental distribution, and salary trends, enabling HR teams to make informed workforce decisions.

🎯 Problem Statement

Employee attrition is a major concern for organizations because it leads to increased hiring costs, loss of experienced employees, and decreased productivity.

The objective of this project is to analyze HR data and answer key business questions such as:

• What is the overall attrition rate in the organization?

• Which departments have the highest employee attrition?

• Which age groups are most likely to leave the company?

• Does salary or job satisfaction influence attrition?

Understanding these patterns helps organizations develop better employee retention strategies.

📂 Dataset

The dataset used in this project is stored in Microsoft Excel (.xlsx) format and contains detailed information about employees and their employment status.

Key Fields in the Dataset

• EmployeeCount – Total number of employees

• Attrition – Indicates whether the employee left the company (Yes/No)

• Age – Age of the employee

• Department – Department in which the employee works

• EducationField – Educational background of the employee

• Gender – Employee gender

• JobRole – Role performed by the employee

• MonthlyIncome – Salary of the employee

• JobSatisfaction – Satisfaction level of the employee

• YearsAtCompany – Total years the employee worked in the organization

This dataset allows analysis of workforce demographics, attrition patterns, and employee satisfaction levels.

🛠 Tools Used

• Microsoft Power BI – Data cleaning, modeling, and dashboard visualization

• Microsoft Excel – Source dataset

🔄 Project Workflow

1️⃣ Data Collection

The HR dataset was obtained in Excel format containing employee demographics, job details, salary information, and attrition status.

2️⃣ Data Import

The dataset was imported into Power BI Desktop for analysis and visualization.

3️⃣ Data Preparation

Data cleaning and preparation steps were performed, including:

• Validating column data types

• Checking for missing values

• Preparing fields required for HR analysis

4️⃣ Data Modeling

The dataset was structured to support calculation of HR metrics such as employee count, attrition rate, and salary distribution.

5️⃣ Dashboard Development

Various visualizations were created to analyze workforce statistics and attrition patterns.

6️⃣ Insight Generation

The final dashboard was used to identify patterns in employee attrition and workforce composition.

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The HR dashboard tracks the following key metrics:

• Total Employees – Total number of employees in the organization

• Total Attrition – Number of employees who left the company

• Attrition Rate – Percentage of employees who left the organization

• Average Age – Average age of employees

• Average Monthly Income – Average salary of employees

• Average Years at Company – Average employee tenure

These KPIs provide a quick overview of workforce stability and employee demographics.
